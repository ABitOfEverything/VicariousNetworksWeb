What does Vicarious Network have to offer?

A Bit More of Everything (Modded Server)

This was our first ground breaking server building the structure that Vicarious Networks was built on, This modpack aims to bring the community together to a modpack where they don't feel the need to add their own mods but can rather just load it up and have all the possible mods they would want.
This modpack is quite intensive a good CPU and 8-16GB Total RAM Recommended.

A Bit More of Everything

was created after A Bit More of Everything to give a more updated community modpack given the lack of modpacks of this style. This modpack comes in at around 280+ Mods and much like it's sister modpack creates a harmonic modpack that players can just load up and not need to add anything that they would have to add to normal modpacks.
Due to the way Minecraft 1.10.2 Works, this modpack requires 8-16GB Total RAM to run efficiently.
